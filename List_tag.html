Ingredients

  • Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Bay Leaves
  • Peppercorns
  • Water
  • Oil

Step by Step Procedure

  1. Marinate the chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, minced garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in the marinade and water. Bring to a boil.
  5. Lower the heat and simmer until the chicken is tender and the sauce is reduced.
  6. Remove the bay leaves and serve hot with rice.
